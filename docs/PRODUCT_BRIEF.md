# notagain — Product Brief

## One-Line Description
A funny, lighthearted entertainment app for wives/girlfriends to enjoy when their partner is on the loo (again).

## Target Audience
Wives, girlfriends, and partners (primarily women 25-45) who find daily domestic humor relatable — especially the "he's disappeared to the bathroom for 30 minutes again" moment.

## Category
Entertainment / Humor

## Revenue Model
TBD — exploring options in Phase 1d

## Key Concept
When your partner vanishes to the bathroom (again), open notagain for a quick dose of relatable humor — memes, stories, interactive content, and a community of people who get it.
