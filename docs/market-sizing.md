# Market Sizing: notagain

**Date:** March 6, 2026
**Phase:** 1c - Market Sizing
**Target Audience:** Women 25-45 in relationships, seeking relatable domestic humor content

---

## Executive Summary

**notagain** is positioned in a blue ocean opportunity within the humor/entertainment app market. This analysis estimates the Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) for a niche entertainment app targeting women 25-45 with relationship/domestic humor content.

**Key Findings:**
- **TAM:** 800M - 1.2B women aged 25-45 globally; $39.2B entertainment app market (2024)
- **SAM:** 150M - 250M smartphone-using women interested in humor content; $8-12B addressable entertainment/humor segment
- **SOM Year 1:** 500K - 2M users (conservative 0.3-0.8% of SAM)
- **Revenue Potential Year 1:** $150K - $2M depending on monetization model

---

## 1. Total Addressable Market (TAM)

### Global Female Population (25-45 age group)

**Baseline Data:**
- Global female population: 4.13 billion (49.7% of world population as of 2026)
- Estimated women aged 25-45: ~800M - 1.2B globally (19-25% of female population based on UN demographic projections)

**English-Speaking Markets Focus (Primary):**
- United States: ~67M women aged 25-45
- UK: ~12M women aged 25-45
- Canada: ~4M women aged 25-45
- Australia: ~3M women aged 25-45
- Other English-speaking markets (Ireland, New Zealand, etc.): ~3M
- **Total English-speaking markets:** ~89M women aged 25-45

**Relationship Status (US data, applicable broadly):**
- In relationships (married or cohabiting): 50-60% of women aged 25-45
- English-speaking markets estimate: ~45-55M women in relationships

### Entertainment App Market Context

**Global Entertainment App Market:**
- Market size (2024): $39.2B in revenue
- Projected growth: 11.9% CAGR through 2030
- Entertainment apps represent ~7% of total app revenue ($330.61B mobile app market)
- 65% of all mobile app downloads are entertainment/gaming category

**Market Opportunity:**
- TAM spans both global (1.2B women) and English-speaking-focused (89M women) opportunities
- Entertainment app market demonstrates sustained growth and consumer engagement
- Humor/meme industry grew from $2.3B (2020) to $6.1B (2025) — 165% growth in 5 years

### TAM Estimate

| Market Segment | Population | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Global TAM** | 800M - 1.2B women 25-45 | UN demographic data; 19-25% of female population |
| **English-Speaking TAM** | 45-55M women in relationships | Primary launch market; 50-60% relationship rate |

**TAM Conclusion:** The market opportunity is substantial. Conservatively, notagain targets 45-55M women in relationships in English-speaking markets, with global expansion potential reaching 800M-1.2B women.

## 2. Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

### Smartphone Adoption & Usage

**Women's Smartphone Penetration:**
- Women aged 25-45 smartphone ownership: ~75% globally
- Smartphone ownership in developed markets: 80-85%
- **Active smartphone users (25-45): ~67-80% of target demographic**

**Daily App Usage (Women 25-45):**
- Women aged 25-34: 102.4 hours/month on apps (~3.4 hours/day)
- Women aged 35-44: 93.6 hours/month on apps (~3.1 hours/day)
- Daily mean across all women: 166.78 minutes (~2h 47m)

**App Category Engagement:**
- Social & communication apps: 50% of time spent
- Video & entertainment apps: 21% of time spent
- Utility & productivity: 14% of time spent
- Other: 15%

### Humor Content Consumption

**Meme & Humor Platform Engagement:**
- TikTok meme engagement: 700M users (70% of 1B TikTok users) actively interact with meme-like videos
- TikTok engagement rate: 2.5% (highest among platforms)
- Instagram meme engagement: 90M+ users monthly
- Gen Z + older audiences: 76% seek comedy content actively
- Humor content uses: 36% use memes to express emotions

**Couple/Relationship Humor Content:**
- Couple comedy hashtags on TikTok: Millions of views on #relationshipcomedy, #couplehumor, #marriagehumor
- Top couple creators: 2-6M+ followers (e.g., Meghan & Jack: 6M followers)
- Engagement rates on couple humor: High, with comedy couples generating millions of views per month
- Reddit + Facebook marriage humor communities: Significant grassroots engagement (exact numbers limited, but "That's It, I'm Wedding Shaming" and similar groups reach hundreds of thousands)

### Comparable Niche App Performance

**Whisper (Anonymous Community App):**
- Peak: 250M+ monthly active users (reported at height)
- Stable: 30-40M monthly active users (current estimates)
- Demographics: Primarily young adults seeking anonymous social connection

**Yik Yak (Niche Humor/Social):**
- Peak (2014): 1.8M downloads in September; 9th most downloaded social app at launch
- Decline: 76% YoY user drop (2015-2016); app shutdown May 2017
- Lesson: Niche social apps require sustained differentiation and viral growth

**Toilet Time (Bathroom-Focused Game):**
- Downloads: 37M (as cited in initial brief)
- Monetization: In-app purchases, ads
- Lesson: Niche apps can achieve significant scale with focused positioning

### Humor App Market Benchmarks

**In-App Advertising Market:**
- Entertainment apps earn significantly less per user than gaming ($3,090/day vs. $22,000/day for games)
- eCPM for entertainment: $2-8 (lower than gaming $10-30)
- Rewarded video: $8-20 eCPM (strong format for humor apps)

### SAM Estimate

| Segment | Market Size | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Smartphone-Using Women (25-45)** | 60-80M (English-speaking markets) | 75% adoption of 80M baseline in primary markets |
| **Entertainment/Humor Content Seekers** | 30-50M (English-speaking markets) | 50-65% of smartphone users engage with entertainment/humor apps |
| **Addressable Entertainment App Market** | $8-12B | 20-30% of $39.2B entertainment market (humor/casual segment) |
| **Comparable Niche Communities** | 30-90M total (across Whisper, Reddit, TikTok couple content, Facebook groups) | Evidence of audience size via existing platforms |

**SAM Conclusion:** Conservative SAM = 30-50M smartphone-using women in English-speaking markets who actively consume humor/entertainment content, with demonstrated engagement on TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, and Facebook. This represents 3-5% of the global TAM and 60-90% of English-speaking primary market.

## 3. Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) - Year 1

### New App Launch Benchmarks

**Year 1 Market Capture for New Apps:**
- Typical new app penetration: 0.1-1% of addressable market in Year 1
- Median time to $1K monthly revenue: 65 days
- Median time to $5K monthly revenue: 120 days
- Apps reaching $1K/month: ~17% of all launched apps
- Top 5% of apps generate 200x more revenue than bottom quartile

**Success Metrics by Revenue Tier:**
- Path to $1K MRR: 17% of apps
- Path to $2.5K MRR: 59% of those reaching $1K
- Path to $5K MRR: 60% of those reaching $2.5K

### Subscription App Conversion Benchmarks

**Free-to-Paid Conversion:**
- Typical freemium conversion: 2-6% (outliers reach 8%)
- Subscription downloads → paying subscribers (30 days): 1.7% median
- Lower quartile: 0.6% | Upper quartile: 4.2%

### Conservative SOM Estimates for notagain

**Scenario 1: Conservative Growth**
- Year 1 downloads: 500K - 800K
- Active users (30-day retention): 150K - 250K
- Market penetration: 0.3-0.5% of SAM
- Rationale: Modest marketing spend, organic growth via App Store optimization, viral loops from couple-humor content sharing

**Scenario 2: Moderate Growth (Base Case)**
- Year 1 downloads: 1M - 1.5M
- Active users (30-day retention): 300K - 500K
- Market penetration: 0.6-0.8% of SAM
- Rationale: Strong product-market fit, influencer partnerships with couple creators, TikTok viral marketing, launch in 2-3 English-speaking markets

**Scenario 3: Optimistic Growth**
- Year 1 downloads: 2M - 3M
- Active users (30-day retention): 600K - 1M
- Market penetration: 1.2-1.8% of SAM
- Rationale: Exceptional viral growth, major influencer partnerships, PR coverage, rapid international expansion

### SOM Conclusion

**Realistic Year 1 SOM: 300K - 500K monthly active users** (Base Case)
- Represents 0.6-1% of SAM, aligned with top-quartile app benchmarks
- Achievable via organic growth + targeted marketing to couple humor audiences
- Requires strong product, viral mechanics, and strategic partnership with relationship/couple content creators

## 5. Revenue Potential Projections

### Monetization Model Scenarios

#### Model A: Ad-Supported (Free App + Banner/Rewarded Ads)

**ARPU Benchmarks:**
- Entertainment apps: $0.35 - $0.77 ARPU/user/year (iOS higher than Android)
- Rewarded video eCPM: $8-20 per 1000 impressions
- Non-gaming app ARPU with ads: $3-15/user/year (industry variance)

**Revenue Projection (500K MAU Base Case):**
- Monthly active users: 500K
- Daily active users (40% retention): 200K
- Monthly impressions (5 ads per user/day): 30M
- eCPM assumption: $3 (lower than gaming due to entertainment category)
- Monthly ad revenue: 30M impressions × $3 CPM / 1000 = **$90K/month**
- **Year 1 estimated revenue: $400K - $600K** (ramping from launch)

#### Model B: Freemium + Subscription

**Subscription ARPU Benchmarks:**
- Netflix ARPU: $7.64 (but much larger platform)
- Disney+ Core ARPU: $6.84
- Generic subscription apps: $5-15/month
- Freemium conversion: 2-6% of users to paid subscribers

**Revenue Projection (500K MAU Base Case):**
- Monthly active users: 500K
- Free-to-paid conversion: 4% = 20K paying subscribers
- Monthly subscription price: $3.99 (competitive for niche entertainment)
- Monthly subscription revenue: 20K × $3.99 = **$80K/month**
- Ad revenue (5% of free users): $20K/month
- **Total Year 1 revenue: $1.2M - $1.5M** (ramping from launch)

**Note:** Subscription ARPU is 4.6× higher than ads-only, making hybrid models more profitable.

#### Model C: Hybrid (Freemium + Ads + In-App Purchases)

**Revenue Projection (500K MAU Base Case):**
- Subscription revenue: $80K/month
- Ad revenue: $50K/month (higher with premium placements)
- In-app purchases (premium features, cosmetics): $20K/month
- **Total Year 1 revenue: $1.5M - $2M** (ramping from launch)

### Revenue Scenario Ranges

| Model | Year 1 Users | ARPU | Monthly Revenue | Annual Revenue | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Ads Only** | 500K MAU | $0.18-0.25/mo | $90-125K | $400-600K | Lower monetization; requires high DAU |
| **Freemium (4% conversion)** | 500K MAU | $0.32/mo | $80K + ads | $1.2-1.5M | Requires compelling premium features |
| **Hybrid (Best Case)** | 500K MAU | $0.40/mo | $200K | $1.5-2M | Balanced approach; industry best-practice |
| **Conservative** | 250K MAU | $0.20/mo | $50K | $150-300K | Slower growth; limited marketing |
| **Optimistic (1M MAU)** | 1M MAU | $0.35/mo | $350K | $3-4M | High growth + viral adoption |

### Revenue Assumptions

**Base Case (Hybrid Model) Assumptions:**
1. 500K MAU by end of Year 1
2. 40% DAU retention (200K daily users)
3. 4% freemium conversion rate to paid subscribers
4. $3.99/month subscription price
5. Ad eCPM: $3 (entertainment category benchmark)
6. 5-8 ad impressions per user per day
7. 10% of free users engage with ads (20K daily ad users)

**Key Drivers for Revenue Upside:**
- Higher DAU retention (50%+ vs 40%)
- Increased freemium conversion (6-8% vs 4%)
- Premium subscription pricing ($4.99-6.99)
- Strong in-app purchase engagement
- International expansion (Year 2+)

## 6. Key Assumptions & Risk Factors

### Critical Assumptions

1. **Market Assumptions:**
   - 45-55M women in relationships in English-speaking markets seeking humor content
   - 30-50M of these are active smartphone users consuming entertainment apps
   - Couple/relationship humor is evergreen, relatable content (not trend-dependent)

2. **Product Assumptions:**
   - notagain delivers compelling, relatable humor content (daily content refresh)
   - Strong in-app viral mechanics (sharing, tagging partners)
   - Retention rates consistent with entertainment apps (30-50% D30)

3. **Distribution Assumptions:**
   - ASO (App Store Optimization) drives 30-40% of early downloads
   - Influencer partnerships with couple creators available and effective
   - Organic word-of-mouth (WOM) drives 40-60% of growth
   - Paid marketing achievable at reasonable CPI ($0.50-1.50)

4. **Monetization Assumptions:**
   - Freemium conversion rates: 2-6% (industry standard)
   - Ad-supported model sustainable with entertainment CPM rates
   - Users willing to pay $3-5/month for premium features

### Risk Factors

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Market Saturation** | High | Position as specific niche (partner-in-loo humor); differentiate from generic couple content |
| **Low Retention** | High | Strong content loop, notifications, partnerships with couple creators for cross-promotion |
| **Ad Fatigue** | Medium | Hybrid monetization reduces reliance on ads; strategic ad placement |
| **Viral Growth Dependency** | High | Build sustainable growth via brand partnerships, influencer relationships, not virality alone |
| **Regulatory (App Store)** | Low | Adult humor content; ensure compliance with app store guidelines for mature content |
| **Conversion Challenges** | Medium | Clearly communicate premium value; freemium feature parity for trial |

## Conclusion

**notagain** operates in a **blue ocean opportunity** with substantial TAM (45-55M English-speaking women in relationships), a demonstrated SAM (30-50M humor/entertainment app users), and a realistic Year 1 SOM of 300-500K MAU. A hybrid monetization model (freemium + ads + IAP) can realistically generate **$1.2-1.5M in Year 1 revenue**, with upside to $2-3M if growth outperforms expectations.

Success depends on **superior content execution**, **strategic influencer partnerships**, and **strong viral mechanics** (in-app sharing, partner tagging). The market is validated by existing platforms (TikTok couple comedy, Reddit marriage humor communities, Instagram meme engagement) and comparable niche app successes (Whisper 30-250M MAU, Toilet Time 37M downloads).

**Risk:** Market saturation and virality dependency. **Mitigations:** Differentiated positioning, sustainable growth loops, hybrid monetization.
